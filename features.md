# Botic Management Application - Features

## Overview

The Botic Management Application is a purpose-built tool for boutique owners who manage custom garment orders. It brings the entire order lifecycle — from recording a new customer's measurements to tracking fabric selection, stitching progress, and final payment — into one organized, easy-to-use platform.

The primary users are **boutique owners** who take custom clothing orders. Instead of juggling paper notebooks, spreadsheets, and photographs stored across multiple devices, this application gives them a single place to manage every customer and every order from start to finish.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Secure Account Access

- **Owner Login**
  - Boutique owners sign in with their **phone number** and a private password to access their account.
  - Each boutique's data is completely private and separate from every other boutique using the platform, ensuring full confidentiality.

- **Profile & Account Management**
  - Owners can update their boutique's display name, change their login phone number, update their email address, and set a new password at any time.
  - All profile changes are protected — updating a password requires confirming the current password first, preventing unauthorized changes.

---

### 2. Customer Management

- **Customer Directory**
  - All customers are stored in a searchable directory. Owners can instantly look up any customer by name or phone number without scrolling through long lists.
  - Business value: Eliminates the need for a physical register or spreadsheet to track who your customers are.

- **Customer Profiles**
  - Each customer has their own profile storing their name, phone number, address, and any personal notes (e.g., fitting preferences, allergies to certain fabrics).
  - Business value: Owners can access a customer's full history and context before a meeting, making every interaction more personalized.

- **Order History per Customer**
  - Viewing a customer's profile shows all past and current orders placed by that customer in one place.
  - Business value: Quickly understand a customer's relationship with the boutique — how many orders they have placed, what they ordered, and current order status — without manual searching.

- **Add & Edit Customers**
  - New customers can be added in seconds with just a name and phone number. Additional details like address and notes are optional and can be filled in later.
  - Existing customer information can be updated at any time.

- **Delete Customers**
  - Customers with no orders can be removed from the directory.

---

### 3. Order Creation & Management

- **Place New Orders**
  - Orders can be created for any existing customer. Each order captures the garment type, the occasion it is being made for (e.g., wedding, casual), and the expected delivery date.
  - Business value: Every order is tied to a customer and an occasion, so there is never any confusion about what is being made and for whom.

- **Supported Garment Types**
  - The application supports eight types of custom garments, each with their own tailored measurement fields:
    - Sherwani
    - Salwar Kameez
    - Kurta Pajama
    - Blazer
    - Lehenga Blouse
    - Suit (3-piece)
    - Nehru Jacket
    - Anarkali Suit
  - Business value: Owners do not need to remember which measurements apply to which garment — the system prompts for exactly the right fields based on what is being stitched.

- **Order Listing & Filtering**
  - All orders can be viewed together, or filtered by their current status (e.g., show only orders that are currently being stitched, or only orders ready for trial). Orders can also be filtered by customer.
  - Business value: Owners can focus on the orders that need attention right now without being distracted by completed or delivered items.

- **Delete Orders**
  - Orders can be permanently deleted when no longer needed.

---

### 4. Measurement Recording

- **Custom Measurement Capture**
  - When an order is created, the owner records the customer's body measurements in inches. The measurement fields shown automatically match the garment type — a Sherwani order shows fields like chest, waist, sleeve, and neck, while a Lehenga Blouse shows bust, waist, and blouse length.
  - Business value: Measurements are stored digitally per order, eliminating the risk of losing handwritten notes and making it easy to reference them at any stage of tailoring.

- **Edit Measurements**
  - Measurements can be updated at any point if adjustments are needed after a trial fitting.

---

### 5. Design Reference Management

- **Upload Design Images**
  - Owners can upload reference photos or design inspiration images directly to an order — photos can be dragged and dropped or selected from a device.
  - Business value: Keeps all design references linked to the specific order, so tailors always know exactly what the garment should look like without physical folders or WhatsApp message hunts.

- **Design Notes**
  - A free-text notes area lets the owner or tailor describe specific design requirements or special instructions for the garment.
  - Business value: Captures verbal instructions from customers in writing, reducing miscommunication between the boutique owner and stitching staff.

- **Manage Design Images**
  - Individual design images can be removed if they are no longer relevant, keeping the order record clean and current.

---

### 6. Fabric Tracking

- **Record Fabric Details**
  - For each order, the owner can record the type of fabric, its color, and where the fabric was sourced from (boutique or supplier name).
  - Business value: Ensures that all fabric information is tied to the order, preventing mix-ups when managing multiple orders with similar materials.

- **Upload Fabric Photos**
  - Photos of the actual fabric can be attached to the order for visual confirmation.
  - Business value: Helpful when coordinating with suppliers or if the customer wants confirmation that the right fabric is being used.

---

### 7. Order Progress Tracking

- **Six-Stage Order Workflow**
  - Every order moves through a clear, visual six-step process:
    1. **Pending** — Order received, yet to begin
    2. **Cutting** — Fabric has been cut
    3. **Stitching** — Garment is being stitched
    4. **Trial Ready** — Ready for the customer to come in for a trial fitting
    5. **Completed** — Final finishing done
    6. **Delivered** — Garment handed over to the customer
  - Business value: Owners always know exactly where each order stands, and can quickly communicate progress to customers without checking with tailors individually.

- **One-Click Status Updates**
  - Moving an order to the next stage takes a single click, making it easy to keep the system up to date even in a busy shop environment.

---

### 8. Photo Documentation

- **Three Categories of Order Photos**
  - Photos can be uploaded against an order in three organized categories:
    - **Customer Photos** — Photos of the customer (for reference during stitching)
    - **Trial Photos** — Photos taken during the trial fitting session
    - **Final Photos** — Photos of the completed garment
  - Business value: Provides a complete visual record of the garment's journey from measurement to delivery, which is useful for resolving disputes, showcasing work, or referencing past styles.

- **Organized Photo Gallery per Order**
  - Photos are displayed grouped by category, making it easy to review the right set of images at each stage of the process.

---

### 9. Billing & Payment Tracking

- **Cost Breakdown**
  - Each order records fabric cost and stitching cost separately. The total order amount is calculated automatically from the two.
  - Business value: Owners know exactly how much an order costs to fulfill and how much to charge the customer, with no manual addition required.

- **Payment Status Tracking**
  - Every order has a payment status — **Paid**, **Partial**, or **Unpaid** — that can be updated as payments are received.
  - Business value: Owners always know which customers have outstanding balances, preventing orders from being delivered without full payment.

---

### 10. Business Dashboard

- **At-a-Glance Overview**
  - The home dashboard gives the boutique owner an instant snapshot of the health of their business:
    - Total number of **active orders** currently in progress
    - Number of orders that are **overdue** (past their delivery date and not yet delivered)
    - Orders **due this week** that need priority attention
    - Number of orders with **unpaid or partial payments**
  - Business value: The owner can walk into the shop every morning and immediately know what needs urgent attention — no need to scroll through a full list of orders.

- **Overdue Orders List**
  - The dashboard surfaces a dedicated list of overdue orders with key details, so the owner can take action immediately.

- **Due This Week List**
  - A focused list of orders that have a delivery deadline within the current week, helping prioritize the day's workload.

---

### 11. Customer Order Tracking Links

- **Shareable Tracking Links for Customers**
  - The boutique owner can generate a secure, token-based link for any order and share it directly with the customer (e.g., via WhatsApp or SMS).
  - The customer can open the link without logging in to see the current status of their order, along with design references, fabric details, and photos.
  - Business value: Eliminates the need for customers to call the boutique for status updates, reducing interruptions during busy tailoring hours.

- **Automatic Link Expiry**
  - Each tracking link expires automatically after **72 hours** from the time it is created, ensuring links do not remain active indefinitely.

- **Manual Link Deactivation**
  - If a link needs to be revoked early (e.g., shared with the wrong contact), the owner can deactivate it immediately from within the order. Deactivation is permanent and cannot be undone.

- **Multiple Links per Order**
  - Multiple tracking links can be created for the same order (e.g., if a new link is needed after the previous one expired), and each can be managed independently.

---

### 12. Tailor Team Tracking Links

- **Shared View for Boutique Staff**
  - The owner can generate a separate tracking link intended for the boutique's tailoring staff. When opened, this link shows **all active orders** in one place — not just a single order.
  - Each order in the tailor view is labeled for urgency: **Overdue**, **Due Soon**, or **Upcoming**, helping the team prioritize work without needing access to the owner's account.
  - Business value: Tailors and assistants can check the full workload and prioritize stitching without the owner having to share login credentials or brief the team individually.

- **7-Day Link Validity**
  - Tailor tracking links are valid for **7 days** by default, reducing the frequency with which links need to be regenerated.

- **Deactivation**
  - Tailor links can be deactivated at any time from the Tailor Tracking Links page, just like customer tracking links.

---

## Additional Notes

- **One Unified Workflow Per Order** — Every order has its own dedicated space that links measurements, design references, fabric details, photos, status tracking, and billing together. Nothing is scattered across separate systems.

- **Instant Search** — Customers can be found in seconds using a name or phone number search, which is especially useful in a busy boutique environment where time matters.

- **Visual Progress Indicators** — Order progress is displayed as a clear visual step-by-step flow, making it easy for anyone — including non-technical staff — to understand the current state of an order.

- **Data Safety & Privacy** — Each boutique's data is fully isolated. A boutique owner can only ever see their own customers and orders, ensuring complete business confidentiality.

- **Mobile-Friendly Design** — The application is designed to work on both desktop and mobile devices, so owners can check order status, update progress, or look up a customer even from a phone on the shop floor.

- **Paperless Operations** — By digitizing measurements, design references, fabric notes, photos, and billing in one place, the application dramatically reduces reliance on paper records, physical folders, and scattered messaging threads.

- **No Public Sign-Up** — New boutique accounts are provisioned through an administrative process, not a self-service sign-up page. This keeps the platform invite-only and prevents unauthorized account creation.
